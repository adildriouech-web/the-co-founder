Privacy & Terms

Plain-language version · last updated 30 June 2026

The Co-Founder is an early-stage tool that helps first-time founders think through decisions. This page explains, honestly and simply, what happens to your data and the terms of using it. No dense legalese.

What we collect

  • Your conversations — but only if you accept the notice on the welcome screen. If you accept, the messages you send and the replies you get are saved so we can learn how to make the product better.
  • An anonymous device ID — a random string kept in your browser so we can count returning users. It isn't linked to your name, email, or identity.
  • Basic usage counts — e.g. number of conversations and thumbs up/down on replies.

We do not ask for or require a name, email, or account to use the app. Please don't paste passwords, card numbers, government IDs, or anything you wouldn't want stored.

Where it's stored

  • Saved conversations live in a secured database (hosted with a third-party database provider) used only by this project.
  • A short running summary of your chats is also kept on your own device (in your browser), so the Co-Founder can pick up where you left off. That summary never leaves your device except as part of your normal messages.

How it's used

Only to run and improve the Co-Founder. It's first-party: we do not sell your data, we do not share it with advertisers, and there are no ad trackers on the site.

How long we keep it

Saved conversations are automatically deleted after 90 days. You don't have to do anything for this to happen.

Your controls

  • Delete everything now: use the "Delete my data" link at the bottom of the app. It erases your saved conversations from the server and clears the summary and history stored on your device. This can't be undone.
  • Clear just this device: clearing your browser storage for the site removes the on-device summary and chat history.
  • Ask a human: email adil.driouech@gmail.com with any privacy request or question, including deletion, and we'll handle it.

Terms of use (the short version)

  • The Co-Founder is an early prototype provided "as is," with no guarantee of accuracy or availability. It can be wrong.
  • It is not professional advice. It is not a lawyer, accountant, financial adviser, or doctor. For legal, tax, financial, or health decisions, talk to a qualified professional.
  • You're responsible for the decisions you make. Use your own judgment and verify anything important.
  • Don't use it for anything illegal, or to submit other people's personal data without their permission.

Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update this page and the date at the top.
