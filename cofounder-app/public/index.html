Welcome — a quick, honest note

The Co-Founder is a calm partner that helps you make a clearer decision and take the next step. Before you start, here's how your data is handled:

  • Your conversations are stored to help improve the Co-Founder.
  • They're also kept on this device so you can return to them.
  • Please don't paste passwords or anything you wouldn't want stored.

This is an early prototype, not a substitute for a lawyer, accountant, or doctor.
